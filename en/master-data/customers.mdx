Customers are the buyers you issue sales invoices and credit notes to. Their
details — including VAT TRN and PEPPOL Participant ID — flow onto your invoices
and enable electronic delivery.

</Frame>

## The customers list

The list shows your customers with **Search** and sortable columns. Each row has
**Edit** and **Delete** actions. Select **Add New** to create a customer.

## Add or edit a customer

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the form">
    Select **Add New**, or **Edit** on an existing customer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the details">
    Complete the sections below, then select **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

  <img src="/images/en/master-data/customers-form-1.png" alt="Fatorly new customer form" />

### Fields

| Section | Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|---|
| Identity | Name (English) | Yes | Legal name in English. |
| Identity | Name (Arabic) | Yes | Legal name in Arabic. |
| Contact | Email | No | Customer email. |
| Contact | Phone | No | Customer phone. |
| Contact | VAT TRN | No | 15-digit UAE Tax Registration Number. |
| Contact | Contact Person | No | Main contact name. |
| Address | Address, City, Country | No | Physical location. Country defaults to `AE`. |
| PEPPOL Network | PEPPOL Participant ID | No | Electronic exchange identifier for direct delivery. |

New customers are **Active** by default.

<Tip>
- Both English and Arabic names are required for e-invoice compliance.
- The VAT TRN must be a valid 15-digit UAE tax registration number.
- Add the PEPPOL Participant ID to enable direct electronic invoice delivery.
</Tip>
