Payment methods are the ways your customers can pay — such as Bank Transfer,
Cash, or card types. You select a payment method when issuing an invoice.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/master-data/payment-methods-1.png" alt="Fatorly payment methods list" />
</Frame>

## The payment methods list

The list shows each method's **Code**, **Name**, and **Status**, with **Search**
and sortable columns. Each row has **Edit** and **Delete** actions.

Fatorly comes with common methods pre-loaded — for example Bank Transfer, Cash,
Check, Credit/Debit Card, Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Diners Club, and
Discover.

## Add or edit a payment method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the form">
    Select **Add New** to create a method, or **Edit** on a row to change one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the details">
    Provide the code and name, then save. New methods are **Active** by default.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Mark methods you don't use as inactive (via **Edit**) to keep the selection list
on invoices short and relevant.
</Tip>
