The **What's New** page (route `/whats-new`) lists release notes and product
updates so you can keep up with new features, improvements, and compliance
changes — in English and Arabic.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/account/whats-new-1.png" alt="Fatorly What's New release notes" />
</Frame>

## What you'll find

Each release entry shows:

- A **title** and **version** (for example, `v2026.07.01`) with the release date.
- **Highlights** summarizing the update.
- Grouped changes under headings such as **New**, **Improved**,
  **Security & Reliability**, and **Compliance**.

## Opening the page

In the sidebar, under **Account**, select **What's New** (`/whats-new`). The most
recent release appears at the top.

<Tip>
Release notes follow your language setting — switch between English and العربية
from your [profile](/en/account/profile) to read them in either language.
</Tip>
