The **Leave** tab of [Company Configuration](/en/settings/company-profile) is where
you start the process of leaving Fatorly. Open it from
**Company → Company Configuration → Leave**.

<Note>
Leaving is an **assisted** process. You begin the request here, and our support
team works with you to complete it — including handing over what you need to keep
and move to another provider. It is not an instant, one-click deletion.
</Note>

## Starting a request

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Leave tab">
    Go to **Company → Company Configuration → Leave**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tell us why">
    Choose a reason and add any detail. This helps us route your request and check
    whether there's anything we can resolve first.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Submit the request. Our team picks it up and contacts you to complete the
    offboarding steps.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What happens to your data

When you leave, you'll be helped to take your records with you, and your invoices
are kept available to you in a read-only / export form so you can meet your
record-keeping obligations.

## What happens to your network participant

If your company is registered on the e-invoicing network, leaving doesn't have to
mean losing your participant identity. As part of offboarding you can be issued a
**migration key** that lets another provider take over your existing participant
ID, so you can keep receiving e-invoices at the same address with your new
provider.

<Tip>
If you're moving to another provider rather than stopping e-invoicing entirely,
mention that when you start your request — the migration key keeps your
participant ID portable.
</Tip>
