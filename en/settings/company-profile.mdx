**Company Configuration** is the hub for everything about the company you're
working in. Open it from **Company → Company Configuration** in the sidebar
(route `/settings/company-profile`). It's organised as a set of **tabs** along the
top — this page covers the hub and its **General** tab, where you edit your legal
and contact details.

## The tabs

The tabs you see depend on your role — some are hidden from auditors, and a few
are only available to a company admin.

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **General** | Legal name, TRN, registration, address, and contact (this page). |
| **Branding** | Logo, brand colour, and what appears on generated documents. See [Branding & Documents](/en/settings/branding-documents). |
| **Number Series** | Document numbering. See [Number Series](/en/settings/number-series). |
| **Usage** | Document counts and plan usage. See [Usage & Statistics](/en/settings/usage). |
| **My Companies** | Companies you have access to. See [Linking Companies](/en/settings/linking-companies). |
| **Team** | Members and auditors. See [Team & Auditors](/en/settings/team-auditors). |
| **Support Access** | Approve temporary support access. See [Support Access](/en/settings/support-access). |
| **Leave** | Request to leave Fatorly. See [Leave Fatorly](/en/settings/leave-fatorly). |

<Note>
**Number Series** and **Usage** are hidden for auditor (read-only) accounts, and
**Team**, **Support Access**, and **Leave** are available only to a company
administrator. If you don't see a tab, your role doesn't include it.
</Note>

## General — company details

The **General** tab holds the legal, tax, and contact details that appear on your
invoices and on the e-invoices you exchange over the network.

### Company identity

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Company Name | Yes | Your legal company name. |
| Tax Registration Number (TRN) | No | Your VAT `TRN`. |
| ID Type | No | Type of your legal registration number (e.g. EID — Emirates ID). |
| Legal Registration Number | No | Your legal registration / licence number. |
| Authority Name | No | The issuing authority. |

### Address & contact

Address, City, Country (defaults to `AE`), Phone, Email, and **Electronic
Address** (used for network routing).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit your details">
    Update any field on the General tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Select **Save** to apply the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Keep your `TRN`, legal registration, and electronic address accurate — they
appear on compliant e-invoices and affect delivery on the network.
</Tip>
