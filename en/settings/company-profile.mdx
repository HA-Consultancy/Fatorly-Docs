Your company profile holds the legal, tax, and contact details that appear on
your invoices, plus your PEPPOL registration and document branding.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/settings/company-profile-1.png" alt="Fatorly company profile" />
</Frame>

## Company Identity

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Company Name | Yes | Your legal company name. |
| Tax Registration Number (TRN) | No | Your VAT TRN. |
| ID Type | No | Type of your legal registration number (e.g. EID — Emirates ID). |
| Legal Registration Number | No | Your legal registration / license number. |
| Authority Name | No | The issuing authority. |

## Address & Contact

Address, City, Country (defaults to `AE`), Phone, Email, and **Electronic
Address** (used for PEPPOL routing).

## PEPPOL Network

Register your company on the PEPPOL network to send and receive e-invoices.

- **PEPPOL Participant ID** — your identifier on the network.
- **Registered with AP** — shows your registration status with the Access Point.
- **Regenerate API Key** / **Unregister** — manage your network registration.

## Document Customization

- **Company Logo** — upload a PNG or JPG (max 2 MB) shown on printed documents.
- **Brand Color** — used on invoice headers and totals.
- **Document Sections** — toggle whether bank details and notes appear on printed
  documents.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Edit your details">
    Update any section.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Select **Save** to apply the changes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Keep your TRN, legal registration, and electronic address accurate — they appear
on compliant e-invoices and affect PEPPOL delivery.
</Tip>
