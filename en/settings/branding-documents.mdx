---
title: "Branding & Documents"
description: "Set your logo, brand colour, and what appears on the invoice PDFs Fatorly generates for you."
---

The **Branding** tab of [Company Configuration](/en/settings/company-profile)
controls how your generated documents look — your logo, your brand colour, and
which sections appear on the invoice PDFs Fatorly produces. Open it from
**Company → Company Configuration → Branding**.

<Note>
This area styles the human-readable PDF Fatorly generates. It does not change the
underlying e-invoice data exchanged over the network — that comes from the
document itself and your [General company details](/en/settings/company-profile).
</Note>

## Logo

Upload a **Company Logo** to show on your printed and downloaded documents. Use a
clear PNG or JPG; a transparent PNG usually looks best on the document header.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose a file">
    Select the logo control and pick your image file.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Select **Save**. New documents you generate will use the updated logo.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
If your logo looks too large or small, re-export it at a higher resolution with
tighter cropping before uploading — the document scales it to fit the header.
</Tip>

## Brand colour

Set a **Brand Colour** to tint document headers and totals so your invoices match
your brand. Choose a colour with enough contrast to stay readable in print.

## What appears on documents

You can toggle which optional sections appear on generated documents — for
example whether **bank details** and **notes** are shown. Turn off anything you'd
rather keep off the printed copy.

<Note>
The exact set of branding controls may vary as the app evolves. The essentials —
logo, brand colour, and document sections — are configured here.
</Note>
