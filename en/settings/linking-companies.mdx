---
title: "Linking Companies"
description: "See and manage the companies you have access to, and switch between them."
---

The **My Companies** tab of
[Company Configuration](/en/settings/company-profile) lists every company your
account can access and lets you move between them. Open it from
**Company → Company Configuration → My Companies** (route
`/settings/my-companies`, which opens the My Companies tab).

<Note>
Your account can have access to more than one company — as the owner, as a linked
auditor, or as support staff. This tab is where you see them all and pick which
one to work in.
</Note>

## Your companies

Each company you can access is shown with:

- The **company name**.
- An **access type** badge — for example **Owner**, **Auditor**, or **Support** —
  so you know the basis of your access.
- A **Current** indicator on the company you're working in now.
- For time-limited access (such as support), the **expiry** of that access.

## Switching and adding

- To change which company you're working in, switch to it from here or use the
  **Switch company** control in the sidebar. See
  [Switching Companies](/en/multi-company/switching-companies).
- To set up another company under your account, use **Add a Company**. New
  companies complete onboarding to link with the FTA, just like your first one.

<Note>
The exact badges and actions available depend on your role and may vary as the app
evolves. Auditor and support access is read-only or time-limited by design.
</Note>
