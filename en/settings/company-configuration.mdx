This page covers the company-level **configuration and defaults** that shape the
documents you create — the settings that apply across your invoices and credit
notes rather than to a single document. They live in
[Company Configuration](/en/settings/company-profile), the tabbed hub for your
company.

<Note>
Configuration is spread across the Company Configuration tabs rather than a single
screen. The exact controls available may vary as the app evolves — use this page
as a map of where each kind of default lives.
</Note>

## Where defaults live

| Setting | Where to set it |
|---|---|
| Legal name, TRN, address, contact | [General](/en/settings/company-profile) |
| Logo, brand colour, document sections | [Branding & Documents](/en/settings/branding-documents) |
| Document numbering and prefixes | [Number Series](/en/settings/number-series) |
| Who can access the company | [Team & Auditors](/en/settings/team-auditors) |

## Defaults that apply to documents

Company-level details flow onto every new document automatically, so you don't
re-enter them each time:

- Your **legal name, TRN, and address** populate the supplier details on each
  invoice.
- Your **electronic address** is used to route e-invoices over the network.
- Your **number series** assign the next document number for each document type.
- Your **branding** (logo, colour, sections) is applied to the generated PDF.

<Tip>
Set these once and keep them current. Accurate company configuration means every
new document starts compliant and correctly branded without extra work.
</Tip>
