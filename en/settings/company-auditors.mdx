---
title: "Company Auditors"
description: "Grant auditors read-only access to your company."
---

This page manages the auditors linked to your company. A linked auditor gets
**read-only** access to your data — useful for accountants or external auditors
who need to review your invoices without making changes.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/settings/company-auditors-1.png" alt="Fatorly linked auditors" />
</Frame>

## The list

Linked auditors are shown with their **Name**, **Email**, **Status**, and
**Linked Date**, with a **Revoke**/remove action. When none are linked, the page
shows an empty state.

## Add an auditor

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Add Auditor">
    Choose **+ Add Auditor**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the auditor's details">
    Provide the auditor's email to send a linking invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auditor accepts">
    Once linked, the auditor can select your company from their
    [auditor view](/en/auditor/company-selector) and review it read-only.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Auditors can view your data but cannot create, edit, or delete documents. Remove
an auditor at any time to revoke their access.
</Note>
