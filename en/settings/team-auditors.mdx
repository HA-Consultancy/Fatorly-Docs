The **Team** tab of [Company Configuration](/en/settings/company-profile) is where
you manage who can access the company you're working in — your **team members**
and your **auditors**. Open it from **Company → Company Configuration → Team**.

<Note>
The Team tab is available to a company administrator. It brings together team
members and linked auditors in one place; the exact controls may vary as the app
evolves.
</Note>

## Team members

Team members are people in your organisation who sign in and work in the company —
creating invoices, managing customers, and so on. Their **role** determines what
they can do.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invite a member">
    Choose to add a member and enter their email to send an invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign a role">
    Pick the role that matches what they should be able to do.
  </Step>
  <Step title="They accept">
    Once they accept the invitation and sign in, the company appears for them.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Auditors

A linked auditor gets **read-only** access to your data — useful for accountants
or external auditors who need to review your invoices without making changes.

Linked auditors are shown with their **Name**, **Email**, **Status**, and
**Linked Date**, with a **Revoke**/remove action. When none are linked, the
section shows an empty state.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add an auditor">
    Choose **Add Auditor**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the auditor's email">
    Provide the auditor's email to send a linking invitation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Auditor accepts">
    Once linked, the auditor can select your company from their auditor view and
    review it read-only.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Auditors can view your data but cannot create, edit, or delete documents. Remove
an auditor at any time to revoke their access.
</Note>
