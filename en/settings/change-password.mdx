Use this page to change your account password while signed in.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/settings/change-password-1.png" alt="Fatorly change password" />
</Frame>

## Change your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the page">
    In the sidebar, under **Settings**, select **Change Password**
    (`/settings/change-password`).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your passwords">
    Enter your current password, then your new password and a confirmation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Submit to update your password. Use it the next time you sign in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Forgot your current password? Sign out and use
[Forgot Password](/en/getting-started/forgot-password) instead.
</Tip>

<Note>
For stronger account security, also enable
[two-factor authentication](/en/getting-started/mfa-setup).
</Note>
