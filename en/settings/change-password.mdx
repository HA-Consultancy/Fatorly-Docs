---
title: "Change Password"
description: "Update the password for your Fatorly account."
---

Change your account password while signed in. Password changes are part of your
personal account, so they live in your [Profile](/en/account/profile) — opening
the older **Change Password** link takes you there.

## Change your password

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile">
    In the sidebar, under **Account**, open your [Profile](/en/account/profile).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your passwords">
    In the security section, enter your current password, then your new password
    and a confirmation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Submit to update your password. Use it the next time you sign in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Forgot your current password? Sign out and use
[Forgot Password](/en/getting-started/forgot-password) instead.
</Tip>

<Note>
For stronger account security, also enable
[two-factor authentication](/en/getting-started/mfa-setup).
</Note>
