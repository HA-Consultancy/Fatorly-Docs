Self-billing is an arrangement where the buyer (you) issues the invoice on behalf
of the supplier. The Self-Billing page manages self-billed tax invoices and
self-billed credit notes.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/invoicing/self-billing-1.png" alt="Fatorly self-billing" />
</Frame>

## The list

The page lists your self-billed documents with their number, date, vendor,
category, total, VAT, and status — with **Search** and sortable columns.

## When to use self-billing

Use self-billing when you have an agreement with a supplier to raise invoices on
their behalf — common for certain procurement arrangements. The documents are
categorized as **Self-billed Tax Invoice** and **Self-billed Tax Credit Note**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a self-billed document">
    Start a new self-billed invoice or credit note from this page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the vendor and add lines">
    Choose the supplier and add line items as you would on a normal invoice.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Review and submit over PEPPOL from the [document view](/en/invoicing/view-invoice).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Self-billing must be agreed with your supplier and meet UAE FTA requirements
before you use it.
</Note>
