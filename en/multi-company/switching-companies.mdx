description: "Choose which company to work in and move between companies you have access to."
---

If your account has access to more than one company, Fatorly lets you pick which
one you're working in and switch between them at any time. The company you select
determines the invoices, customers, and settings you see.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/multi-company/switching-companies-1.png" alt="Fatorly company selector" />
</Frame>

## The company selector

After you [sign in](/en/getting-started/login), users with access to multiple
companies land on the **company selector** (route `/select-company`) instead of
going straight to the dashboard.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review your companies">
    Each company you can access is shown as a card with its name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a company">
    Select the company you want to work in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Continue to the dashboard">
    Fatorly opens that company's [dashboard](/en/home/dashboard). Everything you
    do from here — invoices, customers, reports — applies to the company you
    chose.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
If your account has access to a single company, you skip this screen and go
straight to the dashboard after signing in.
</Note>

## Switching company later

You don't have to sign out to change companies. Use the **Switch company**
control in the sidebar to return to the selector and pick a different company.

<Tip>
The company you're currently working in is shown in the sidebar, so you always
know which company your actions affect.
</Tip>

## Adding a company

Select **+ Add a company** on the selector to start setting up another company
under your account. New companies complete [onboarding](/en/getting-started/onboarding)
to link with the FTA, just like your first one.
