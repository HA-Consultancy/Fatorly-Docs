The **Profile** page (route `/profile`) is where you manage your personal
account — your name and contact details, your language and theme preferences, and
your account security. Open it from **Account** in the sidebar.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/account/profile-1.png" alt="Fatorly user profile page" />
</Frame>

## Your details

The profile shows the basic information tied to your user account:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| Name | Your display name. |
| Email | The email you sign in with and where account notifications are sent. |

<Note>
This page covers your personal user account. Settings that apply to your business
live under **Company** — see [Company Profile](/en/settings/company-profile).
</Note>

## Preferences

Choose how the app looks for you:

- **Language** — switch between **English** and **العربية**.
- **Theme** — switch between light and dark mode.

Your choice is remembered for your account.

## Security

From the profile area you can keep your account secure:

- **Change password** — update your password while signed in. See
  [Change Password](/en/settings/change-password).
- **Two-factor authentication (MFA)** — the entry point for setting up an
  authenticator app for an extra layer of security. See
  [MFA Setup](/en/getting-started/mfa-setup).

<Tip>
Enabling two-factor authentication is the single best step you can take to
protect your account.
</Tip>
