---
title: "Sales Credit Notes"
description: "Manage the outbound credit notes you issue to customers to correct or reverse a sales invoice."
---

A **credit note** corrects or reverses an invoice you previously issued — for
example for a return, a discount, or a pricing correction. The **Sales Credit
Notes** page lists the credit notes you issue to your customers. Open it from
**Sales → Credit Notes** in the sidebar.

## The list

Each row is one credit note. The grid has these columns:

- **Invoice #** — the credit note number from your
  [number series](/en/settings/number-series) (for example `CN-2026-00008`).
  Select it to open the document.
- **Issue Date** — the date the credit note was issued.
- **Buyer** — the customer the credit note was issued to.
- **Referenced Invoice** — the preceding sales invoice the credit note relates
  to (for example `INV-2026-00002`).
- **Total** — the value being credited in `AED`.
- **Status** — a badge such as **Draft** or **Sent**.
- **Actions** — see [Row actions](#row-actions) below.

Above the grid, the **All documents** chip shows the full count, **Total Value**
shows the combined credited value in `AED`, and **Pending Submission** filters to
credit notes not yet submitted. Use **Search** to find a document and select a
column header to sort.

## Create a credit note

Two actions in the top-right open the [editor](/en/invoicing/create-invoice):

- **New Tax Credit Note** — a VAT credit note.
- **New Commercial Credit Note** — a commercial (non-tax) credit note.

A credit note is filled in the same way as an invoice — choose the customer, add
line items, and set VAT groups. Record the **referenced invoice** so the credit
note is correctly tied to the document it corrects.

<Tip>
Issue a credit note rather than editing an invoice you have already submitted.
Credit note amounts reduce the customer's outstanding balance.
</Tip>

## Row actions

- **View** is always available and opens the [document
  view](/en/invoicing/view-invoice), where you can review and submit the credit
  note over Peppol.
- **Delete** appears on **Draft** credit notes that have not yet been submitted.
