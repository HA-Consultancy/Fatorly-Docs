The login page is where you access your Fatorly account using your email address
and password.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/getting-started/login-1.png" alt="Fatorly sign-in page" />
</Frame>

## Sign in

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the login page">
    Go to your Fatorly URL, or navigate to `/login`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your credentials">
    Type your registered email address and password. The **Sign In** button
    activates once both fields are filled.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Sign In">
    You are taken to your [dashboard](/en/home/dashboard). If two-factor
    authentication is enabled on your account, you are prompted for your
    authenticator code first — see [MFA Setup](/en/getting-started/mfa-setup).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fields

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Email Address | Yes | The email you registered with. |
| Password | Yes | Your account password. |

## Switching language and theme

The top of the page has controls to switch between **English** and **العربية**
and to toggle light/dark mode. Your choice is remembered on this device.

<Tip>
First time here? You need to [verify your email](/en/getting-started/verify-email)
before your first sign-in.
</Tip>

## Trouble signing in?

- **Forgot your password?** Select the link below the form — see
  [Forgot Password](/en/getting-started/forgot-password).
- **No account yet?** Select **Create one** — see
  [Create an Account](/en/getting-started/register).
