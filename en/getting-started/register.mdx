The registration page is where new users create a Fatorly account. After you
register, Fatorly sends a verification email — you confirm your address before
signing in for the first time.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/getting-started/register-1.png" alt="Fatorly Create Account page" />
</Frame>

## Create your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the registration page">
    Go to the [sign-in screen](/en/getting-started/login) and select **Create
    one**, or navigate directly to `/register`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your details">
    Fill in your full name, email address, and a password. Optionally add your
    company name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the security check">
    If a security check is shown, complete it. The **Create Account** button
    stays disabled until the required fields are filled and the check passes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit">
    Select **Create Account**. Fatorly creates your account and sends a
    verification email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email">
    Open the email and confirm your address. See
    [Verify Email](/en/getting-started/verify-email) for details. You can resend
    the email from the confirmation screen if it does not arrive.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Fields

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| Full Name | Yes | Your name as the account owner. |
| Company Name | No | Your business name. You can also set or change this later in [Company Profile](/en/settings/company-profile). |
| Email Address | Yes | Used to sign in and to receive the verification email. Must be a valid address you can access. |
| Password | Yes | Your account password. |

<Tip>
Use an email address you check regularly — account verification, password
resets, and important notifications are sent there.
</Tip>

<Note>
Registering creates your user account only. To send and receive invoices over
the PEPPOL network, complete [onboarding](/en/getting-started/onboarding) to link
your business with the UAE Federal Tax Authority (FTA).
</Note>

## Already have an account?
