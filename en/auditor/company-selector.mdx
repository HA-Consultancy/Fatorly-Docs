title: "Auditor: Select a Company"
description: "Choose which linked company you want to review."
---

If your account has the **auditor** role, you sign in to a dedicated company
selector instead of a single company's dashboard. From here you choose which of
your linked companies to review.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/auditor/company-selector-1.png" alt="Auditor select a company to audit" />
</Frame>

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get linked to a company">
    A company adds you as an auditor from their
    [Company Auditors](/en/settings/company-auditors) settings. Once linked, the
    company appears in your list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in with your auditor credentials. You land on **Select a Company to Audit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a company">
    Each card shows the company name, its account email, and the date you were
    linked. Select a card to open that company in read-only mode.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Switch any time">
    Use **Switch Company** inside a company to return here and pick another.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The company selector screen is shown in English.
</Note>

<Tip>
Don't see a company you expect? Ask them to confirm they've added your email under
**Settings → Auditors**.
</Tip>
