After selecting a company, you see that company's data in **read-only** mode. The
layout matches the normal Fatorly experience, with an **Auditing** banner so you
always know which company you're reviewing.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/auditor/auditing-a-company-1.png" alt="Auditor reviewing a company dashboard" />
</Frame>

## What you can see

The sidebar shows an **Auditing — &lt;Company Name&gt;** banner with a **Switch
Company** button. You can browse the company's:

- **Dashboard** — totals, VAT, submission status, recent activity.
- **Sales** and **Purchases** — invoices and credit notes.
- **Self-billing** documents.
- **Master Data** — items, customers, vendors, payment methods.
- **Company Profile** and other read-only settings.

Open any document to review its details, line items, and totals.

## Read-only access

<Warning>
As an auditor you have **read-only** access. You cannot create, edit, delete, or
submit documents, and create/manage actions are not available. To review a
different company, select **Switch Company**.
</Warning>

<Tip>
Use the same language and theme toggles as the main app to review in English or
Arabic, light or dark.
</Tip>
