Fatorly helps UAE businesses create, send, and receive tax-compliant electronic
invoices over the PEPPOL network, in line with UAE Federal Tax Authority (FTA)
requirements. This guide walks you through every part of the redesigned app.

## Finding your way around

The redesigned app keeps everything in a left sidebar, grouped by what you're
doing:

- **Dashboard** — your [at-a-glance overview](/en/home/dashboard) of invoices,
  VAT, and submission status.
- **Sales** — outbound [invoices](/en/sales/sales-invoices) and credit notes you
  issue to customers.
- **Purchases** — invoices and credit notes you receive from suppliers.
- **Self-billing** — documents you raise on a supplier's behalf.
- **Reports** — curated reports and the report builder.
- **Master Data + Files** — [customers](/en/master-data/customers), vendors,
  items, payment methods, and your uploaded files.
- **Company** — your [company profile](/en/settings/company-profile), number
  series, integrations, and team.
- **Account** — your [profile](/en/account/profile), security, and the
  [What's New](/en/home/whats-new) changelog.

Users with access to more than one company also get a
[company switcher](/en/multi-company/switching-companies) in the sidebar.
