**Self-billing** is an arrangement where the buyer — you — issues the invoice on
the **supplier's behalf**, rather than waiting for the supplier to send it. The
**Self-billing** page is where you create and manage self-billed tax invoices and
self-billed credit notes. Open it from **Self-billing** in the sidebar.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/en/self-billing/self-billing-1.png" alt="The Fatorly Self-billing list" />
</Frame>

## When to use self-billing

Use self-billing when you have a written agreement with a supplier to raise
invoices on their behalf — a common arrangement in certain procurement
relationships. Because you are issuing on the supplier's behalf, the **supplier**
is shown on each document rather than a buyer.

<Note>
Self-billing must be agreed with your supplier and meet UAE FTA requirements
before you use it.
</Note>

## The list

Each row is one self-billed document. The grid has these columns:

- **Invoice #** — the document number from your
  [number series](/en/settings/number-series) (for example `SBI-2026-00001` for
  an invoice or `SBCN-2026-00001` for a credit note). Select it to open the
  document.
- **Issue Date** — the date the document was issued.
- **Category** — *Self-billed Tax Invoice* or *Self-billed Tax Credit Note*.
- **Supplier** — the supplier the document is raised on behalf of.
- **Total** — the total in `AED`.
- **Status** — a badge such as **Draft** or **Sent**.
- **Actions** — **View** opens the [document
  view](/en/invoicing/view-invoice).

Above the grid, the **All documents** chip shows the full count, **Total Value**
shows the combined value in `AED`, and **Pending Submission** filters to
documents not yet submitted. Use **Search** to find a document and select a
column header to sort.

## Create a self-billed document

Two actions in the top-right open the [editor](/en/invoicing/create-invoice):

- **New Self-billed Invoice** — a self-billed tax invoice.
- **New Self-billed Credit Note** — a self-billed credit note.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the document">
    Choose **New Self-billed Invoice** or **New Self-billed Credit Note**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the supplier and add lines">
    Choose the supplier the document is raised for and add line items as you
    would on a normal invoice, setting quantities, prices, and VAT groups.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and submit">
    Review the totals, then submit over Peppol from the [document
    view](/en/invoicing/view-invoice).
  </Step>
</Steps>
